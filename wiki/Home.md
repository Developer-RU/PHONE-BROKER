## What Is PHONE BROKER?

PHONE BROKER is an iOS Flutter application that runs embedded MQTT broker instances directly on iPhone for local development, diagnostics, and demo scenarios.

## Key Capabilities

- Multi-broker management with unique ports
- Runtime controls (start/stop/restart)
- Client/session diagnostics and logs
- Import/export for broker configurations
- Network diagnostics and app-level settings
